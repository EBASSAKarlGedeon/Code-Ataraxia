# NSF — Neurovore Suppression Force

An international military coalition tasked with defending humanity from Neurovores and containing large-scale threats. Each nation operates its own branch under a unified global command structure.

### NSF Rank Structure

This is the formalized military hierarchy of the **Neurovore Suppression Force (NSF)**. Because the NSF operates globally to contain anomalies and Class-5 Hollows, their ranking system balances traditional field command with the operational demands of coordinating high-level Actuants and SAS networks.

#### Commissioned Officers (Command & Strategy)

- **General:** The absolute highest strategic command rank. Responsible for theater-wide operations, international defense coordination, and deployment policies for Void Hunters.
    
- **Major General:** The second-highest command rank. Oversees regional headquarters and large-scale operational fronts (e.g., managing containment zones near the Dark Wall).
    
- **Lieutenant General:** High-level field commander directing multiple operational divisions or specialized strike forces.
    

#### Field Grade Officers (Tactical Deployment)

- **Colonel:** Commands entire frontline bases, massive fortified defense hubs, or high-tier Actuant regiments.
    
- **Lieutenant Colonel:** Manages standard combat wings, logistics pipelines, and multi-unit tactical deployments.
    
- **Major:** Serves as tactical director for primary field deployments, ensuring synchronization thresholds are maintained across active units.
    

#### Company Grade Officers (Frontline Leadership)

- **Captain:** Directs specific field companies. Often acts as the primary link between frontline ground units and centralized SAS command arrays.
    
- **First Lieutenant:** Experienced frontline officer leading active squads during containment missions.
    
- **Second Lieutenant:** The entry-level commissioned officer rank. Fresh graduates from the academies who are given their first formal field assignments.
    

#### Trainee Status (Legacy/Cadet Pipeline)

- **Cadet:** A trainee studying to become a commissioned officer at elite institutions like Zenith Academy.
    
    - _Status:_ Cadets occupy a unique institutional grey area. They hold authority superior to all Junior Enlisted personnel but remain strictly subordinate to all commissioned officers (beginning at Second Lieutenant).
        
    - _The Goal:_ Graduation from the training program triggers an automatic promotion to Second Lieutenant, officially entering the formal rank structure.
        

#### Junior Enlisted Ranks (Day-to-Day Operations & Frontline Grid)

- **Specialist:** Experienced personnel handling technical operations, localized network stabilization, or specialized support roles.
    
- **Private First Class:** Standard operational grunt handling routine patrols, local security details, and perimeter defense.
    
- **Private:** The absolute lowest rank in the NSF. New recruits handling basic maintenance, manual labor, and initial field placement.
    

### Narrative Integration for Kevin

Given this exact hierarchy, Kevin’s position as an **NSF Cadet** puts him in a tight spot in Arc One:

- He is technically higher in status than the hardened Privates and Corporals who have been fighting Stage 1 and 2 Neurovores for years in the muddy trenches, which can create instant resentment.
    
- At the same time, any green Second Lieutenant fresh out of a corporate academy can completely boss him around, forcing him onto dangerous vanguard or decoy assignments.
