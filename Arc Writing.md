### Arc 1: Genesis (Part 1 Timeline)

**Setting the Anchor:** October 14th, 2250. Kevin Kavallier turns 18.

- **Scene 1: Frontline Familiarity (The Patrol)**
    
    - _Focus:_ Establishing the core dynamic. After a grueling day of physical and neural synchronization training, Kevin and **Nagi Karman** are assigned to routine perimeter patrol.
        
    - _Beat:_ Nagi brings his signature optimistic energy, using the walk to confidently complain about his training loops while transparently fishing for advice on how to talk to **Naomi Randall**. Kevin's role as the grounded, reliable companion is established.
        
- **Scene 2: Gathering the Vanguard (Seto Platoon Assembly)**
    
    - _Focus:_ Group dynamics and character traits. Kevin and Nagi return to the local staging point to meet back up with the rest of the unit under **Captain Seto Narukami**.
        
    - _Beat:_ Introductions showcase the core dynamics: **Rin Asakura** running up with her usual energetic warmth, subtly checking Kevin for injuries; **Yuuki Miyasaki** standing calmly nearby, his handsome, gentle presence anchoring the group; and Naomi quietly logging data, offering Kevin a refined smile that sets off Nagi's panic. Seto briefs them with casual concern, emphasizing that everyone comes back alive.
        
- **Scene 3: Tactical Execution (The Stage 3 Threat)**
    
    - _Focus:_ Action and CAS mechanics. The platoon receives a sudden alert regarding a localized anomaly: a dangerous Stage 3 Neurovore has broken the outer cognitive energy barriers.
        
    - _Beat:_ The squad puts their teamwork on display. Seto locks the monster down with high-voltage _Electrokinesis_; Nagi manipulates the wind currents using _Aerokinesis_ to redirect the monster's projectiles; Naomi uses _Short-term Precognition_ to call out blind spots; and Yuuki utilizes his _Sword Dance_ state to dismantle its primary limbs. Kevin delivers the fatal strike using _Hypervelocity_ combined with his Black Katana, cleanly cutting the core.
        
- **Scene 4: The Calm Before the Breach (Tokyo HQ)**
    
    - _Focus:_ The unrequited emotional stakes. Back at the high-tech Tokyo Headquarters, the adrenaline fades.
        
    - _Beat:_ Kevin and Rin share a quiet, personal moment away from the barracks. The scene carries a distinct, bittersweet "patrol date" atmosphere. Rin tries desperately to act casual while keeping her burning feelings hidden, letting slip how much she relies on him, while Kevin remains focused on their duty as cadets.
        
- **Scene 5: The Fall of Kanto (The First Death)**
    
    - _Focus:_ The catastrophic rug-pull. The alarm blares across the Japanese Sector. A massive Stage 4 Neurovore emerges from the Wakayama Bay, prompting a desperate distress call from the Osaka branch. Seto and the heavy hitters are immediately diverted to contain the coastal threat.
        
    - _Beat:_ The diversion is perfect. A massive, unexpected explosion rips through the heart of the Tokyo Fortress Hub—a targeted attack from within the surveillance grid.
        
    - _Impact:_ Reacting purely on instinct, Kevin activates _Hypervelocity_ to throw himself over Rin, using his own body to shield her from the concussive heat blast. The blast knocks Rin unconscious but shatters Kevin's body, leaving him bleeding out in the smoke-filled rubble.
        
    - _The End:_ Multiple Stage 2 Neurovores crawl out from the devastation, swarming the ruins. Unable to move, torn between the physical impossibility of carrying Rin away and the sheer horror of his broken limbs, Kevin is brutally overwhelmed. His vision redlines, his neural network goes dark, and he dies.
        
- **The Rewind:**
    
    - Kevin snaps awake gasping for air, clutching a chest that isn't wounded.
        
    - _The New Reality:_ October 14th, 2247. He is 15 years old, sitting intact inside his room at the traditional Kavallier Residence in the Kyoto Sector. The three-year loop has begun.
