### [CHARACTER_ROSTER: SETO PLATOON]

#### 1. Nagi Karman

- **Appearance:** Brown hair, gold-hazel eyes. Wears a signature utility belt around his outfit. Black combat pants with black and grey mission boots.
    
- **Personality:** Highly optimistic, energetic, and fiercely loyal. One of Kevin's closest friends since their entry into the NSF. Strives to maintain platoon morale while harboring a massive, obvious crush on Naomi Randall.
    
- **Personal Weapon:** Chakram
    
- **CAS Diagnostic:** K2–E3 / C3 (Stable CSR, Multi-field KBI)
    
    - _Domains:_ Atmospheric Physics, Fluid Dynamics, Pressure Manipulation.
        
    - _CAS Ability:_ **Aerokinesis** (Wind manipulation and atmospheric currents control).
        

#### 2. Rin Asakura

- **Appearance:** Auburn red hair tied back firmly with a signature red hairband, purple eyes. Opts for the specialized _Makoto Variant_ of the standard Black Battle Attire: a long jacket with one long sleeve, a short tactical skirt over form-fitting shorts, combat gloves, and a utility belt.
    
- **Personality:** Bright, relentlessly energetic, and an exceptionally hard worker. Possesses an intense hatred for injustice, often rushing headlong into dangerous situations before thinking. Extremely kind at her core, she habitually suppresses her own distress or deeper feelings to avoid burdening the platoon. Deeply in love with Kevin, a fact she desperately tries to conceal.
    
- **Personal Weapon:** Torch
    
- **CAS Diagnostic:** K3–E4 / C2 (Vulnerable CSR, Combat-Focus KBI)
    
    - _Domains:_ Thermal Dynamics, Molecular Acceleration, Combustion Physics, Spatial Projection.
        
    - _CAS Ability:_ **Pyrokinesis** (Thermal manipulation and flame generation).
        

#### 3. Naomi Randall

- **Appearance:** Refined blond hair and clear blue eyes. Wears a distinct, handmade hairclip matching the one she gifted to her younger sister, Kasane.
    
- **Personality:** The oldest daughter of the elite Randall family—the masterminds behind Randall Industries (a major military contracting sector of **Pharos Dynamics**). Raised in immense luxury but entirely un-spoiled, she treats everyone with equal, pure kindness and a strong sense of justice. Deeply protective of her adopted sister Kasane. Possesses a quiet, elegant, and slightly shy personality that makes her highly popular, though she remains unattached—holding a growing interest in Kevin due to his innate charm and kindness.
    
- **Personal Weapon:** Handgun
    
- **CAS Diagnostic:** K4–E2 / C4 (Highly Sensitive CSR, Analytical KBI)
    
    - _Domains:_ Temporal Calculations, Neural Network Synchronization, Probability Matrices, Sensory Processing.
        
    - _CAS Ability:_ **Short-term Precognition** (Visual foresight of immediate future outcomes).
        

#### 4. Yuuki Miyasaki (Future Void Hunter)

- **Appearance:** Striking short blue hair and calm blue eyes. Possesses a handsome, highly comforting presence that immediately puts people at ease. Wears a black hooded tactical jacket, long black pants, grey strapped combat boots, and black gloves.
    
- **Personality:** The absolute embodiment of kind justice, driven by a deeply distorted, near-broken sense of values. He derives self-worth _only_ from helping others without seeking compensation, viewing the act of saving someone as its own reward. He believes survival inequality is inherently unfair. He will gladly sacrifice his limbs, health, or life for a stranger without a second thought, causing deep worry among his friends. When facing absolute, irrational evil, his gentle demeanor flips into a cold, terrifyingly violent persona. Obsessed with the cartoon _Starlight Knights_.
    
- **Personal Weapon:** Black Longsword (Artifact)
    
- **CAS Diagnostic:** K5–E2 / C1 (Critical Threshold CSR, Multi-field KBI)
    
    - _Domains:_ Biomechanics, Kinetics & Dynamics, Neural Acceleration, Spatial Awareness, Energy Circulation, Predictive Cognition, Sensory Augmentation, Pattern Recognition.
        
    - _CAS Ability:_ **Sword Dance**
        
    - _Sword State:_ Intakes massive oxygen volumes to accelerate blood flow, heart rate, and body temperature. Excites muscles and bones to grant superhuman strength, speed, stamina, durability, and hyper-accelerated cognitive processing.
        
    - _Sword Sense:_ An advanced form of extrasensory perception that reads an opponent's raw will to fight. Allows him to instinctively spot vitals, determine threat levels at a glance, predict incoming actions, and adapt continuously to combat styles.
        

#### 5. Captain Seto Narukami (Platoon Commander)

- **Appearance:** Eloquent and cheerful young man with short blond hair and sharp blue eyes.
    
- **Personality:** A deeply humble, highly skilled officer who routinely lends a hand to anyone in need with casual, nonchalant concern. He likes where he works and has refused all promotions, so his rank isn't much to brag about, but he attracts people to him with his charming character. Due to the nature of service in the NSF, people die in battle. However, Seto's strong sense of responsibility means he does everything in his power to keep those serving under him alive. It seems this behavior is the product of his inherent kindness and some hard experiences he's had in the past.
    
- **Personal Weapon:** Kodachi
    
- **CAS Diagnostic:** K4–E4 / C3 (Stable CSR, Tactical Master KBI)
    
    - _Domains:_ Electromagnetism, Particle Acceleration, Ionization Physics, Synaptic Overload Matrices.
        
    - _CAS Ability:_ **Electrokinesis** (High-voltage electrical manipulation and current control).
