### Proposing Ideas & Mechanical Expansion

#### 1. Defining the CAS Codes: "K2–E3 / C3"

To make his scientific CAS profile look highly professional and diagnostic, we can define what these letters and numbers stand for:

- **K (Knowledge Capacity / Omniscience Score):** **K2** represents "Advanced Operational." He has high theoretical knowledge in his specific domains (Kinematics, Dynamics, Spatial Awareness) but doesn't possess the massive, multi-disciplinary, encyclopedic mind of a pure scientist (which would be K5).
    
- **E (Execution Potency / Omnipotence Score):** **E3** represents "High Kinetic/Force Output." His capability to push energy into the physical world is highly efficient, allowing him to bypass normal physical limits to move at sonic/hypersonic speeds.
    
- **C (Cognitive Stability Rate):** **C3** represents "Vulnerable." A standard operational status under mild stress, but highly susceptible to emotional volatility or memory corruption if pushed beyond his threshold or if he loops too many times.
    

#### 2. The Mechanics of his "Black Katana"

- Since standard metal would melt, bend, or shatter due to air resistance and kinetic friction when Kevin uses Hypervelocity, his blade shouldn't just be an ordinary sword.
    
- **Idea:** The blade is a **Class-3 Artifact** designed by his grandfather Karson Kavallier. It uses a CAS-conductive carbon weave that absorbs the static energy and heat generated by Kevin's high-speed movement and channels it along the edge, turning the friction that should kill him into a superheated, razor-sharp cutting edge.
    

#### 3. Framing his Initial NSF Role

- In Arc One, Kevin should start as a scout or a vanguard candidate. Because his Hypervelocity is incredibly fast but lacks raw, wide-scale destructive output (unlike his mother’s time control or highest-class energy blasts), his superiors might relegate him to "reconnaissance" or "decoy" duty.
    
- This immediately reinforces his starting underdog status—he is incredibly fast, but in a world of giant monsters and massive corporations, speed without massive physical force is often undervalued, fueling his personal determination to prove himself before he ever dies.
